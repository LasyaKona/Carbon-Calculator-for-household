🌍 Why Carbon Footprint Matters

A household carbon footprint is the sum of emissions caused by daily life:

🔌 Electricity – Appliances, lighting, and gadgets
🚗 Transport – Cars, buses, motorcycles, even flights
🍽 Food – What we eat, especially meat and processed food
🔥 LPG Gas – Cooking gas used in cylinders for everyday meals

EcoTrack helps you measure this and gives actionable tips to go greener.
